About Me

Aarushi, ECE Student

Hello, I'm Aarushi

I am an Electronics and Communication Engineering undergraduate with interests in core electronics, sustainability, and research-oriented learning. I enjoy applying engineering concepts to real-world problems, especially where technical work can lead to meaningful and practical outcomes.

Through my academic journey so far, I have had the opportunity to contribute to published research and assist in the editing of an academic book, experiences that have helped me develop a deeper appreciation for careful analysis, technical clarity, and structured thinking. I have also worked on sustainability-focused projects, including those related to environmental challenges, which continue to influence the direction of my interests.

I approach my work with consistency and focus, often spending long hours learning, experimenting, and improving my understanding. This portfolio is a growing reflection of my academic progress, projects, and evolving interests.
